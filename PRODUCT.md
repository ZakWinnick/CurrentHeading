# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

People arriving from a Current Heading video, photograph, podcast episode, or social post who want to understand Zak Winnick's work and find the channel, Instagram, podcast, membership, or shop. Most visits are short, mobile, and discovery-led.

## Product Purpose

Present Current Heading as Zak's personal media brand across three parallel lanes: EV ownership, EV charging and infrastructure, and aviation. The site should establish the point of view through real work, make recent media easy to find, and offer quieter paths to Ground Loop, community support, and merchandise.

## Brand Personality

Observant, direct, and grounded. Smart without smugness, technically precise when warranted, dryly funny, and visibly made by a filmmaker and photographer rather than a corporation.

## Anti-references

- Loud creator-economy funnels that ask for money before showing the work.
- Credential-heavy corporate biographies.
- Generic AI landing pages with glass panels, gradient text, repeated cards, or manufactured editorial styling.
- Forced EV and aviation crossover concepts.
- Retired Wyld Wattage language or public aviation-training claims.

## Design Principles

1. Establish the brand and creator clearly before presenting its live destinations.
2. Keep EV ownership, charging and infrastructure, and aviation equally valid without forcing crossover.
3. Let one visual moment be bold, then create quiet intervals.
4. Keep monetization available but secondary to discovery.
5. Make claims only when current sources support them.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. Preserve browser zoom, visible keyboard focus, semantic landmarks and headings, meaningful accessible names, reduced-motion behavior, 44px touch targets where practical, and at least 4.5:1 contrast for normal text.
